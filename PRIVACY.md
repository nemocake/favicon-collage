# Privacy Policy

**Favicon Collage** does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

The extension runs entirely on your device. It reads your browser history and your locally cached favicons only to draw a collage inside the extension's own page. This happens in your browser, and the result is never sent anywhere. There is no server, no analytics, and no third party code.

Your choice of which sites to include in a collage is saved locally through the browser's own storage and never leaves your machine.

## Permissions

- **history** is used to list the sites whose favicons make up the collage. It is only read on the page and never transmitted.
- **favicon** is used to load the icon already cached by your browser for each site.
- **storage** remembers which sites you chose to hide, on your device only.
